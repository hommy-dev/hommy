### Notes on the offer (why the copy reads this way)
- No "limited / tight per city / leads not spread thin" claims — they contradict the real broad-fan-out model and would burn trust. The honest angle is "homeowner picks by quality, so show up and win."
- "Pay only when you win" is the hook, but the body states **$1 (1 credit) to start a conversation** accurately — no bait-and-switch.
- Engage cost is 1 credit ($1); real fee only on a win; the $300 free credits cover hundreds of conversations.
- CAN-SPAM footer (mailing address + unsubscribe) on both emails.
